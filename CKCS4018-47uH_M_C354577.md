## Important Design Notes & Limitations

- **Current derating required:** Irms of 420 mA is defined at ΔT = +40°C from 25°C ambient. At elevated ambient temperatures (>85°C), derate current to maintain safe operating temperature.
- **Saturation margin:** Peak inductor current (DC bias + AC ripple) must remain below **570 mA** (Isat). Exceeding Isat causes >30% inductance drop, risking converter instability.
- **DCR tolerance:** DCR specified at ±30% variation — account for worst-case 845 mΩ in efficiency/loss calculations.
- **SRF limitation:** Minimum SRF is 10 MHz; keep switching frequency well below this (recommended <2 MHz for stable inductive behavior).
- **Frequency range:** Inductance tested at 100 kHz/1 Vrms — verify inductance at actual operating frequency if significantly different.
- **Reflow soldering:** SMD tape-and-reel packaging (3000 pcs/330 mm reel); use standard SMD reflow profiles compatible with lead-free solder.
- **PCB footprint:** Use recommended pad pattern F=1.4 mm, G=1.3 mm, H=3.7 mm (center-to-center) per Fig 3 of datasheet.
